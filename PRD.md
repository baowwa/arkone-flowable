# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Overview
The Arkone Flowable laboratory management system is designed to streamline laboratory processes, enhance data management, and improve compliance with regulatory standards.

## Target Audience
- Laboratory Managers
- Researchers
- Quality Assurance Personnel

## Key Features
- Sample tracking
- Inventory management
- User role management
- Reporting and analytics

## User Stories
- As a laboratory manager, I want to track samples to ensure they are properly processed.
- As a researcher, I want to manage inventory to know what materials are available.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The system should allow users to create, read, update, and delete samples.
- The system should generate reports based on user queries.
